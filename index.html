About me

Being a Software Engineer based in Belém, Brazil, has its privileges: the nature here are wonderful.

But I have worked mainly remotely in the last few years, developing Web applications and sites for startups and organizations of all sizes — helping them to establish a refined digital presence.

Building useful and performatic digital products across the multiple devices and browsers. Is what I do =)

I’m experienced in the front-end development (JavaScript, React, Angular, HTML, CSS, SVG, accessibility, responsive design), Git, CI/CD and I’m going deeper on the back-end (Node.js, PHP, RESTful APIs, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Firebase, AWS, Microsoft Azure).

Now I’m working at TCM-PA with public data focused projects, learning more about software engineering and in free time making open source PWAs <3
