About Me

My name is Lucas Mauess, I am a Web Developer and UX/UI Designer. I work with development of websites and applications for small and medium businesses, personal customers, and as well, as partner or consultant to agencies and starter projects.

I specialize in front-end development, and I mostly focus on simplicity, ergonomics, responsive design, cross-browser compatibility and SEO-friendly code. I am currently working as a freelancer

I'm from Brazil and I live in Belém — the capital and largest city of the state of Pará, in the country's north — on middle of Amazon, the largest rainforest of the world. I love living here (is not a jungle), the same way as I love traveling and discover new challenging experiences \o/

I love the worlds of Web/Mobile Development and UX/UI Design, especially when I work together with a creative team. I combine my knowledge, my skills and my experience to deliver the best project possible, always, with an eye for innovation.

I am a enthusiast for open source technologies, mainly those that make the free and open web. And currently I am studying at Federal University of Pará (UFPA) to undergraduate in Computer Science.

I'm also co-founded the INBLUG, an startup specializing in the design and development of interactive projects of websites and mobile applications. But I created the INBLUG, not only thinking in offer these services, but also in possibility to develop the projects of everyone, who do not know, how to transform your ideas into practice. Just, the INBLUG mission is more than design and develop, but convert a great idea into reality :)
