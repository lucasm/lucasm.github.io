About Me

My name is Lucas Mauess, I am a Web Developer and Web Designer. I work with development of websites, webapps and UI/UX design project for mobile applications and digital products. I am currently working as a freelancer for small and medium businesses, personal customers, and as well, as partner or consultant to agencies and starter projects.

I specialize in front-end development, with focus on responsive design, acessibility, cross-browser compatibility, SEO-friendly and scalable code. With an eye for innovation, I combine my knowledge, skills and experience to deliver the best product possible. And I really like the possibility of working together with creative people and awesome teams.

I'm Brazilian and I live in Belém, on middle of the Amazon (the largest rainforest of the world). But here is not a jungle, is the capital and largest city of the state of Pará, in the country's north. Here I study to undergraduate in Computer Science at Federal University of Pará (UFPA). And the same way I love living here, I like to travel and discover new challenging experiences, not only in the world, but also in the world wide web :)
